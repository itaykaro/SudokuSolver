# SudokuSolver
Sudoku puzzle solver using backtracking.

First, fill in the board, for example:
```
||=====================================||  
|| - | - | 1 || - | - | - || - | 6 | - ||   
|| - | - | - || 8 | - | - || - | - | - ||   
|| - | 7 | 3 || - | - | - || - | 1 | 9 ||  
||=====================================||  
|| - | - | 5 || - | - | 2 || - | - | 7 ||  
|| - | - | - || 7 | 6 | - || 5 | 8 | - ||  
|| 7 | - | 4 || 5 | 9 | - || - | - | 6 ||  
||=====================================||  
|| - | 6 | - || - | 2 | - || - | - | - ||  
|| - | - | - || - | - | - || - | 7 | 8 ||  
|| 1 | 9 | - || - | - | 3 || 2 | 4 | - ||  
||=====================================||  
```
Then, the solver will print the correct solution:
```
SOLVED!
Solution:
||=====================================||
|| 8 | 5 | 1 || 3 | 4 | 9 || 7 | 6 | 2 ||
|| 9 | 2 | 6 || 8 | 7 | 1 || 3 | 5 | 4 ||
|| 4 | 7 | 3 || 2 | 5 | 6 || 8 | 1 | 9 ||
||=====================================||
|| 6 | 8 | 5 || 1 | 3 | 2 || 4 | 9 | 7 ||
|| 2 | 1 | 9 || 7 | 6 | 4 || 5 | 8 | 3 ||
|| 7 | 3 | 4 || 5 | 9 | 8 || 1 | 2 | 6 ||
||=====================================||
|| 5 | 6 | 8 || 4 | 2 | 7 || 9 | 3 | 1 ||
|| 3 | 4 | 2 || 9 | 1 | 5 || 6 | 7 | 8 ||
|| 1 | 9 | 7 || 6 | 8 | 3 || 2 | 4 | 5 ||
||=====================================||
```
